Who We Are

We are a member of the Pacific Northwest Conference of the United Church of Christ, located in beautiful Wallace Idaho.

The United Church of Christ is a national denomination of many thousands of churches across the US from many traditions committed to the biblical call that they may all be one (John 17:21). As a congregational church, we believe with the Pilgrim Fathers that Christ is the only head of the church, and so each church is autonomous (self-ruling).

Our mission: to love our neighbor as ourselves, to make disciples, and to worship the triune God.
